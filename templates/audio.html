Audio Security Center

Encrypt Audio

Protect your audio files (MP3, WAV, AAC, etc.) from unauthorized access.

  1. Select your audio file.
  2. Click "Encrypt".
  3. Wait for the 3D encryption process.
  4. Download your secured file.

Decrypt Audio

Restore your encrypted audio files to their original playable format.

  1. Upload the encrypted file (usually ends in .txt).
  2. Click "Decrypt".
  3. Wait for the verification process.
  4. Download your original audio.
